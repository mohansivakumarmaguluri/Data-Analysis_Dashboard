InsightEdge: Revenue & Profit Dashboard

Project Objective:
To design an interactive dashboard using Python and Excel that analyzes global revenue, net profit, and operational margins (2024–2025) across regions and service lines, providing clear KPIs, growth metrics, and actionable insights for business decision-making.

##Question(KPI's)

1.Which region generated the highest revenue growth from 2024 to 2025?

2.Which service line contributed the largest net profit in 2025?

3.How did the overall Operating Profit Margin (OPM) change year-over-year?

4.Which service line showed the strongest balance of revenue and profit growth?

5.Which region demonstrated the most consistent profitability across months?
